## About This Project

The Portfolio Risk Analyzer helps delivery leaders maintain visibility across their entire portfolio by automatically identifying patterns that indicate emerging risks.

### Key Features (Planned)
- Automated risk pattern detection
- Portfolio-wide risk heat map
- Trend analysis and early warning indicators
- Mitigation recommendation engine
